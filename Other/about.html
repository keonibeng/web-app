About this System

This Web Application is a user-friendly and visually appealing application that can give more productivity for the users. This application is a Requesting A Purchasing Favor System with a tagline “Request for more, we buy for sure”. This system is a simple and efficient solution for requesting and managing personal shopping favors

Have you ever needed to purchase something but couldn't find the time to go out and buy it yourself? Or maybe you don't have access to certain stores or online marketplaces? Whatever the reason, our system is designed to make it easy for you to request a shopping favor from our trusted shopping helpers or Shoppers.

With our system, you can create a shopping request specifying the item(s) you need, the quantity, and any other relevant details with just a few steps. The request is then sent to our shopping helpers that you have been designated. They can then review the request and decide whether or not they are able to fulfill it. You will receive your requested shopping items from the date or schedule that you have been selected from the application.

Whether you need someone to pick up groceries, buy a birthday gift, or simply help you shop for a special occasion, our "Requesting A Purchasing Favor" System makes it easy to get the help you need, without the hassle of doing it all yourself.

This application came up with the idea when last year, I was not feeling well and lying in the bed for the whole day, my mom sometimes forgets to buy some of my medicine. I fully understand her situation because, she has a lot of things to do in our house. I told her “I wish there is a service like, if we forgot something to buy, we can just ask for it and there is someone who will buy for us.” and she just smile and agree from my speech. With that, our Requesting A Purchasing Favor System invented.

Purpose

  • The purpose of this system is to cater and accommodate people to buy their needs without stepping out their doors, especially who has no service vehicle, insufficient amount of money to travel or stuck in the house due to bad weather.

  • This system provides a convenient and organized way for individuals to request and manage personal shopping favors. This system aims to simplify the process of obtaining items that are difficult for individuals to purchase on their own due to time constraints, limited access to certain stores or marketplaces, or other reasons.

  • The system allows users to create shopping requests specifying the item(s) they need and any other relevant details. The requests are then sent to designated shopping helpers, who can review and accept them. This enables individuals to receive the help they need without having to spend time or resources shopping themselves.

  • In addition to making, it easier for individuals to obtain necessary items, the system also facilitates communication and collaboration between the requester and the shopping helpers. Users can track the status of their requests, receive notifications when they are accepted and fulfilled, and leave feedback for their shopping helpers.

Goals and Objective

This system specifically aims to:

  • Help people to buy their needs when the weather is not in good condition

  • Cater people who currently at the hospital which cannot buy their needs immediately

  • Cater individuals who is alone in their respective household while having an illness or feeling sick

  • Accommodate people who doesn’t have service vehicles to buy their needs in the city or living far away from the city

  • Be an alternative service vehicle for those individuals which their own car or vehicle has a hardware damage, lock of or empty fuel or flat tier

  • Help employees to buy their needs immediately for they can’t go outside of their establishment or institution due to obeying proper schedule of time

  • Cater fast-food restaurants or shops to buy their needs when their items or resources is about to few or low especially, when they are well-known in their current places

The objectives of this system include:

  • Convenience: To provide a convenient and time-saving solution for individuals who need to obtain items that are difficult for them to purchase on their own

  • Accessibility: To enable individuals who do not have easy access to certain stores or marketplaces to still obtain the items they need

  • Organization: To provide a system that facilitates organized communication and collaboration between the requester and the shopping helpers, including the ability to track the status of requests and leave feedback.

  • Trustworthiness: To establish a sense of trust and reliability between the requester and the shopping helpers, by providing a platform that ensures clear communication and transparency.

  • Efficiency: To streamline the process of requesting and fulfilling personal shopping favors, reducing the time and effort required for both the requester and the shopping helpers.

  • User satisfaction: To ensure that the system meets the needs and expectations of its users, by providing a user-friendly interface and gathering feedback to improve the system over time.
